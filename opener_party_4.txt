The college party was in full swing, but the real action was happening upstairs. Taylor, the host, had invited her three closest friends to her bedroom. There was Chris, her boyfriend, already sitting on the bed. Next to him was her roommate Ashley, who'd been eyeing Chris all night. And then there was Ryan, Ashley's ex, who'd shown up unexpectedly. The four of them found themselves alone in the dimly lit room, the music from downstairs providing the perfect cover for what was about to happen...
