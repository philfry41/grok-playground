The bachelorette party was supposed to be a tame affair, but things had taken an unexpected turn. The bride-to-be, Amanda, sat on the hotel bed with her four bridesmaids: her sister Brooke, her best friend Nicole, her college roommate Vanessa, and her cousin Tiffany. The stripper they'd hired, a gorgeous woman named Destiny, had just arrived and was already setting up her equipment. The champagne was flowing, inhibitions were lowering, and the five women found themselves in a situation none of them had anticipated...
