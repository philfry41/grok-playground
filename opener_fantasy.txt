The magical realm was in chaos, and the six chosen heroes had been summoned to save it. There was Aria, the elven mage, her long silver hair flowing around her shoulders. Next to her stood Thorne, the human warrior, his muscles glistening with sweat from battle. The dwarf cleric, Gimli, was checking his healing potions while the halfling rogue, Pip, sharpened her daggers. The half-orc barbarian, Grok, was already removing his armor, and the tiefling sorcerer, Zara, was casting a spell to create a private sanctuary. The six of them knew they needed to bond their powers together to defeat the dark lord, but the ritual required more than just magic...
