The office was empty except for the three of them working late. Jennifer, the new intern, was filing papers when she noticed her boss, Mr. Thompson, watching her from his office. His wife, Lisa, who also worked at the company, was typing at her desk nearby. Jennifer felt their eyes on her as she bent over to reach the bottom drawer.
